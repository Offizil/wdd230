Membership level

Non-Profit Membership

Cost: N15,000 per year

Benefits:

  • Inclusion in Chamber publications as a non-profit supporter.
  • Access to networking events and business mixers.
  • Listing in the online Chamber directory.
  • Participation in community outreach programs.
  • Opportunity to promote non-profit events through Chamber channels.

Bronze Membership

Cost: N30,000 per year

Benefits:

  • All Non-Profit Membership benefits.
  • Priority access to Chamber-sponsored training and workshops.
  • Exclusive discounts on Chamber events and seminars.
  • Recognition as a Bronze Member in Chamber communications.
  • 10% discount on advertising services, including spotlights on the Chamber's home page.

Silver Membership

Cost: N52,000 per year

Benefits:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Featured spotlight position on the Chamber's home page.
  • Enhanced visibility in Chamber publications.
  • Complimentary access to select premium Chamber events.
  • 15% discount on advertising services, including featured spots on the Chamber's website.

Gold Membership

Cost: N85,000 per year

Benefits:

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • VIP access to exclusive Chamber events and forums.
  • Prominent logo placement in Chamber materials and publications.
  • Customized promotional opportunities tailored to your business.
  • 20% discount on advertising services, including premium spots on the Chamber's home page.
