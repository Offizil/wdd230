WDD 230

Overview

Site Name

The name of the site will be "Calabar Chamber of Commerce".

Site Purpose

Calabar Chamber of Commerce is a dynamic and influential organization committed to advancing the interests of businesses within our community. Established 2024, we have been a driving force in promoting commerce, collaboration, and community development. Our mission is to create a supportive environment for businesses of all sizes, offering a platform for growth, networking, and advocacy.

Scenarios

scenario 1

User X: An existing chamber member interested in participating in a networking event in Calabar.

  • The user logs into their member account on the website.
  • They check the "Events" page to find upcoming networking events.
  • The user registers for an event, receives confirmation, and accesses additional details such as venue and agenda

scenario 2

User Y: A local entrepreneur looking to start a new business in Calabar..

  • The user visits the Calabar Chamber of Commerce website.
  • They navigate to the "Membership" section and find information on how to register a new business.
  • The user follows the provided guidelines, submits necessary documents, and completes the registration process online.

scenario 2

User M: A business owner seeking professional development opportunities.

  • The user explores the "Training" section on the website.
  • They find information about upcoming workshops and training sessions.
  • The user registers for a relevant session, pays the fee online, and receives confirmation.
